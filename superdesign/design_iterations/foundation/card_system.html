Card Usage Guidelines

✅ Do's

  • • Use cards to group related content
  • • Keep card content focused and concise
  • • Use headers for titles and actions
  • • Use footers for actions and metadata
  • • Maintain consistent spacing and alignment
  • • Use status cards for system information

❌ Don'ts

  • • Don't nest cards within cards
  • • Don't overload cards with too much content
  • • Don't use cards for simple text content
  • • Don't mix different card types randomly
  • • Don't forget to handle empty states
  • • Don't use cards for navigation elements
