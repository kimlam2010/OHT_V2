## 🚨 **CRITICAL RISKS (HIGH PRIORITY)**

### **Risk #1: E-Stop System Failure**
- **Probability:** Medium (30%)
- **Impact:** Critical (Safety violation)
- **Risk Level:** HIGH
- **Mitigation:**
  - Dual-channel E-Stop implementation
  - Hardware redundancy
  - Regular safety testing
  - Emergency procedures training

### **Risk #2: RS485 Communication Failure**
- **Probability:** Medium (25%)
- **Impact:** High (System functionality)
- **Risk Level:** HIGH
- **Mitigation:**
  - Redundant communication paths
  - Error detection và correction
  - Network monitoring
  - Failover procedures

### **Risk #3: Performance Latency Issues**
- **Probability:** High (40%)
- **Impact:** Medium (User experience)
- **Risk Level:** MEDIUM
- **Mitigation:**
  - Performance optimization
  - Load testing
  - Real-time monitoring
  - Scalability planning

### **Risk #4: Compliance Violations**
- **Probability:** Low (15%)
- **Impact:** Critical (Legal/Regulatory)
- **Risk Level:** HIGH
- **Mitigation:**
  - Regular compliance audits
  - Standards monitoring
  - Documentation review
  - Expert consultation
