# 📚 **TRAINING SCHEDULE - DOCUMENTATION SYSTEM**

## 🎯 **MỤC TIÊU**

Lịch trình training chi tiết cho việc triển khai hệ thống tài liệu OHT-50 Master Module cho tất cả teams.

## 📅 **TRAINING TIMELINE**

### **Day 1: System Deployment**
- **09:00-10:00:** System deployment và validation
- **10:00-11:00:** Backup và rollback testing
- **11:00-12:00:** Access permissions setup
- **14:00-15:00:** System validation testing
- **15:00-16:00:** Issue resolution và fixes

### **Day 2: Leadership Training**
- **09:00-10:30:** CTO & PM Teams Training
- **10:30-12:00:** Team Leads Training
- **14:00-15:30:** EMBED & Firmware Teams Training
- **15:30-17:00:** Q&A và hands-on practice

### **Day 3: Development Teams Training**
- **09:00-10:30:** Backend & Frontend Teams Training
- **10:30-12:00:** QA & DevOps Teams Training
- **14:00-15:30:** Cross-team coordination training
- **15:30-17:00:** Final validation và feedback

### **Day 4-5: Validation & Monitoring**
- **09:00-12:00:** System validation testing
- **14:00-17:00:** Team feedback collection
- **Daily:** Issue resolution và support
